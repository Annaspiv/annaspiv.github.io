Strawberry cheesecake

  • Time: 1 h
  • Serves: 8 person
_Ingredients_ _Quantity_
berry puree 300 g
curd cheese 500 g
cream 33% 300 g
sugar 170 g
gelatinr 20 g
cookies 240 g
butter 150 g

Step 1: Grind the cookies into crumbs and pour over the melted butter. Mix, put in a detachable form, tamp well and put in the refrigerator.

Step 2: Grind berries (strawberries) with a blender and pass through a sieve. Add sugar and gelatin to the berry puree, let it swell.

Step 3: On low heat, heat until the gelatin dissolves and allow to cool.

Step 4: Whip the cream until firm peaks, add curd cheese and beat well again.

Step 5: Add berry puree, mix. Pour over the sand base and send to the refrigerator. Grease the edges of the form (sides) with butter.

Step 6: It is best to leave the cheesecake overnight.

Frozen strawberry yogurt

  • Time: 3 h
  • Serves: 2 person
_Ingredients_ _Quantity_
peeled strawberries 400 g
natural yogurt without additives 300 g
sugar 100 g
lemon juice 1-2 tbsp. l.

Step 1: Grind the berries with sugar or powdered puree. Add yogurt and blend again. Add lemon juice to taste and stir.

Step 2: Pour the mass into an ice cream maker and freeze for about 30 minutes.

Step 3: Pour the mass into an ice cream maker and freeze for about 30 minutes.
